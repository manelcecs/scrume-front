Scrume is a tool to apply Scrum into a project in a complete, simple and assisted way.

Here you can keep up with the agile methodology without losing any of the documentation that Scrum generates in the course of the Sprints.

Kanban board, list of automated tasks, reminders of Scrum processes, linking with Git.

With Scrume you will have the power of a Scrum Master in your hand.
